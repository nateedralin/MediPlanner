About MediPlanner

MediPlanner is a simple health tracking tool that helps users calculate their daily calorie needs and plan for wellness. Whether you're trying to lose weight, maintain your energy, or manage your health routines, MediPlanner gives you a starting point.

Built with a focus on usability and clarity, MediPlanner combines modern web design with practical health tools — right in your browser.
