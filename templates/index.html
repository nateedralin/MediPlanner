What is Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)?

Your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) estimates the amount of calories your body burns at rest in order to perform essential functions, such as breathing and circulation. This BMR calculator helps you find that calorie number based on your given height, weight, age, gender, and overall how active you are. 'Your resting BMR' shows the number of calories your body burns in order to stay alive. These calories support essential & vital functions such as digestion, circulation, and breathing. 'With activity level' factors in daily movement, whether that be walking or exercising. This calorie amount is your total daily energy expenditure (TDEE). This shows how many calories you need to maintain your current weight based on your lifestyle.
