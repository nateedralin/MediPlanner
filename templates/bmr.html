What is Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)?

Your resting BMR shows the number of calories your body burns in order to stay alive. These calories support essential & vital functions such as digestion, circulation, and breathing. 'With activity level' includes your resting BMR plus the calories your body burns from your daily activity (like walking, working out, or just moving around).It’s your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) — the number of calories you need to maintain your current weight based on your lifestyle.
